# Cost Allocation and Timekeeping Plan

## Compliance Baseline

- Every cost charged must be **allowable** and consistent with the award's terms, supported by documentation (2 CFR 200)
- For federal dollars, salary charges must be supported by records reflecting actual work performed and internal controls; estimates alone are not sufficient as final support (2 CFR 200.430)
- Subrecipient monitoring must be explicit once OJJDP is involved; DOJ requires monitoring consistent with 2 CFR 200.332 and verification that subrecipients are not excluded
- TxGMS alignment with federal Uniform Guidance requires separate identification of awards and source/application of funds

## Timesheet Rules (Audit-Ready, Role-Based)

These rules satisfy 2 CFR 200.430 documentation standards for when the OJJDP award enters.

### Rule 1 - Daily Capture, Pay-Period Certification

- Staff record time at least daily by Grant Fund + Cost Objective
- Supervisor reviews and approves by end of each pay period
- Staff certifies time is true/complete

### Rule 2 - One Cost Objective Per Entry

- No "admin" catch-all
- Every entry must map to a cost objective (CO-01 through CO-09)

### Rule 3 - No Double-Charging

- The same hour cannot be billed to more than one Grant Fund
- If a role works on multiple funds, the distribution must be supported by time entries reflecting actual work performed

### Rule 4 - Allocation for Shared Activities

- Shared meetings must be charged to the cost objective that benefits the meeting's purpose
- If a meeting supports multiple grants, split by documented agenda segments OR prohibit splitting and charge to one grant with written justification

## Documentation Checklist (Minimum Set)

### Financial + Procurement

- [ ] Approved budget and budget narrative per award
- [ ] General ledger with Grant Fund + Cost Objective coding
- [ ] Payroll register + paystubs
- [ ] Timesheets with certification + supervisor approval
- [ ] Invoices, receipts, and proof of payment for all non-payroll costs
- [ ] Procurement documentation (quotes/bids) [VERIFY award requirements]
- [ ] Subrecipient agreements + scope of work + monitoring plan
- [ ] Subrecipient invoices + supporting detail + approval records

### Program Delivery Proof

- [ ] Referral logs (court/probation source, date, eligibility)
- [ ] Intake and consent documentation
- [ ] Screening records (tool name [NEEDED if not in OAFC package]; results stored securely)
- [ ] Case notes/contact logs (90-day cadence)
- [ ] MOUD linkage records (referrals, appointment kept confirmation, follow-up)
- [ ] Naloxone training + distribution logs + inventory controls
- [ ] Family Partner logs (education sessions, home visits, supports provided)
- [ ] YRC attendance logs + engagement tracking
- [ ] Training logs (JI-RPS / peer training pathway; staff training)

### Reporting

- [ ] OAFC quarterly reports and required OAFC performance metrics outputs
- [ ] For DOJ/OJJDP: JustGrants progress/performance reports (semiannual: Jan-Jun due Jul 30; Jul-Dec due Jan 30)
- [ ] For DOJ/OJJDP: SF-425 financial reports (quarterly within 30 days; final within 120 days of award end)
- [ ] Evidence of on-time submission for all required reports
