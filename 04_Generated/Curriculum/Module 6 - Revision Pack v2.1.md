## 1. 🔧 Redesign the Decision Lab — trauma-safe + forward-focused (priority)

> **Replace the prompt.** Instead of "share a tough choice you faced / how would S.P.A.U.S.E. have changed that night":
>
> **New facilitator script:** "We're going to put S.P.A.U.S.E. to work — your call how. You can use **one of these scenario cards**, OR a situation you've seen or heard about, OR something from your own life. You don't have to share details, and you don't have to share at all. We're not here to dig up regrets — we're building the move you'll make **next time**."
>
> - **Always offer the hypothetical/scenario option** so no one has to disclose.
> - **Forward frame:** "What's your S.P.A.U.S.E. play next time this comes up?" (not "what went wrong").
> - Optionally surface a **win:** "Or tell us about a moment you already paused and made the call that protected your Blueprint."

> **Facilitator note (for heavy disclosures):** "If someone shares something heavy: don't probe, validate it ('that took guts to say'), bring it back to the skill, and check in **privately** after. Know your mandatory-reporting obligations before this module." 

## 2. Learning Objectives — ADD to "Facilitator Overview"

By the end of Module 6, participants will be able to:
1. **Recall** the 6 steps of S.P.A.U.S.E. (Stop, Predict, Assess, Understand, Select, Exit). *(Understand)*
2. **Apply** S.P.A.U.S.E. to a high-pressure scenario, step by step. *(Apply)*
3. **Predict** the "ripple" consequences of a choice on self and community. *(Analyze)*
4. **Connect** S.P.A.U.S.E. to their S.T.R.O.N.G. refusal skills at the "Exit" step. *(Apply)*

## 3. Formalize the check + Exit Ticket

- **Role-play rubric (Activity 3):** group correctly walks all **6 steps** and uses a S.T.R.O.N.G. exit = mastery of Objectives 2 & 4.
- **Exit ticket (~3 min):** (1) List the S.P.A.U.S.E. steps; (2) One ripple a good choice creates for someone you care about; (3) Where you'll keep your pocket card. → Objectives 1, 3.

## 4. ADD a "How My Tools Connect" one-pager (reduce framework load)

A single visual: **Gas Pedal/Brakes (M3)** → when an urge hits, **5-Second Override (M3)** or **5-4-3-2-1 (M5)** to buy time → **S.P.A.U.S.E. (M6)** to decide → **S.T.R.O.N.G. (M4)** to exit → **Coping Menu (M5)** for the Healthy High. One map, all the tools.
