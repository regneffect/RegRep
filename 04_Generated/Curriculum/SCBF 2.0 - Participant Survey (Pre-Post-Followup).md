# SCORING KEY — STAFF ONLY (remove before youth copies)

**Match** every form by the secret code (pre ↔ post ↔ follow-up).

### Knowledge (items 1–5) — score # correct (0–5)
- 1 = **T** · 2 = **F** · 3 = **T** · 4 = **T** · 5 = **All of the above**
- Report **% correct, pre vs post.** *Target: +20 pts or ≥80% post.*

### Refusal self-efficacy (items 6–8) — PRIMARY OUTCOME
- Score = **mean of items 6–8** (1–5).
- Report pre → post → 6-month. *Target: **≥ +1.0** pre→post, **maintained** at 6-month.*

### Coping / regulation (9–11) & Future orientation / intentions (12–14)
- Score = mean per cluster (1–5). Report pre → post change.

### Program feedback (15–16) & open items (17, follow-up Q3)
- Descriptive: mean for 15–16; theme the open responses.

### Reporting
- Per cohort: knowledge % gain, self-efficacy mean change, coping/intentions change, 6-month maintenance.
- **Pick your headline metric before the cohort starts** — recommended: *refusal self-efficacy gain* + *% completing a SMART goal* (from the Module 1 exit ticket).

### Notes
- Item 2 is written so **False = correct** (don't reverse-score by accident).
- Confidence/agreement items are not yet from a validated scale — fine for internal tracking; validate before formal evaluation/publication.
