# Connect the Dots — Journalism Pipeline

Priority 5 in [[Claude]]. Accessible, sharp public writing for Black community papers and broader audiences — connecting policy to everyday life. This is the **drafting workbench**: idea → outline → draft. Polish and production happen in the Power by Design publishing session (see Handoffs).

> **Voice:** grounded, direct, useful. Strength-based language. Claims must be verifiable; strong but legally careful. **RIS stays internal** — never named in public copy unless Reggie asks.

## Standing rules

- Connect policy to everyday life — that's the brand.
- Every claim verifiable; cite receipts in the outline.
- Legally careful: strong, not reckless.
- Strength-based framing (peer-led, lived-experience leadership), not deficit framing.
- RIS / Racialized Incentive State: internal analysis only.
