## Non-Negotiable Compliance Rules

1. **OAFC** prohibits duplicate reimbursement: grant funds may not be used for costs reimbursed by another funding source (NOFA Para 140)
2. **OJJDP** (federal): allowability, allocability, reasonableness, and documentation per 2 C.F.R. Part 200 Subpart E; internal controls per 2 CFR 200.303
3. **Hogg TCBRI**: funds non-clinical capacity building ONLY; cannot fund direct service delivery; award capped at 30% of operating budget; applicant operating budget must be under $250,000
4. **No cost** may be charged to more than one funder for the same work product, service hour, or deliverable

## Critical Non-Duplication Rules

1. **Same youth, same hour**: A service hour for a youth enrolled in both OAFC and OJJDP cohorts must be charged to ONE funder only, based on the service type and enrollment pathway
2. **Naloxone**: 100% OAFC. Do not charge to OJJDP or Hogg.
3. **Evaluation**: 100% OJJDP. Do not charge to OAFC or Hogg.
4. **Training Coordinator**: 100% Hogg (non-clinical capacity). Do not charge to OAFC or OJJDP.
5. **Shared costs** (office space, data systems, Reggie's time): Must have documented allocation basis in funder_firewall.md with supporting timesheets/records
6. **Hogg cannot fund**: Direct clinical services, service delivery hours, anything that OAFC or OJJDP already funds
