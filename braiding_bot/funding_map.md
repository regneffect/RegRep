# Braided Funding Map: OAFC vs. Hogg vs. OJJDP

## Non-Negotiable Compliance Rules

1. **OAFC** prohibits duplicate reimbursement: grant funds may not be used for costs reimbursed by another funding source; grantees must demonstrate other funds are not paying costs charged to OAFC. (Source: Grants_104_NOFA_FINAL.docx Para 140)
2. **Federal dollars** (OJJDP) require allowability, allocability, and documentation (2 CFR 200.403, 200.405) and internal controls (2 CFR 200.303).
3. **Hogg** does not accept unsolicited proposals; Hogg lane details remain [NEEDED] until the exact opportunity is specified.
