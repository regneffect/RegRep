# Funder Firewall: Cost Allocation Rules

> **Last Updated**: March 7, 2026
> **Purpose**: Prevent double-billing by documenting exactly how each shared cost is allocated across funders. This is the operational companion to funding_map.md — that file shows WHAT each funder pays for; this file shows HOW shared costs are divided and WHERE the documentation lives.

---

## Governing Standards

| Funder | Cost Standard | Key Requirement |
|---|---|---|
| OJJDP | 2 C.F.R. Part 200 Subpart E | Costs must be allowable, allocable, reasonable, and consistently treated; timekeeping per 2 CFR 200.430 |
| OAFC | TxGMS / OAFC NOFA | Grant funds may not be used for costs reimbursed by another funding source (NOFA Para 140) |
| Hogg TCBRI | Hogg Foundation grant terms | Non-clinical capacity building only; award ≤ 30% of operating budget; operating budget < $250K |

## Red Lines (Never Cross)

1. **Never** charge naloxone to OJJDP or Hogg
2. **Never** charge clinical service delivery to Hogg
3. **Never** charge the same staff hour to two funders
4. **Never** charge evaluation costs to OAFC or Hogg
5. **Never** charge OJJDP-required travel/conferences to OAFC
6. **Never** exceed Hogg's 30% of operating budget cap
7. **Never** submit OAFC reimbursement without verifying the cost is not reimbursed elsewhere
8. **Never** charge Hogg Training Coordinator time for direct service delivery
