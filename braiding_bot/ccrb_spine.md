## Program Logic (What CCRB Interrupts)

Intercepts youth at court processing / probation decision points (Youth SIM Intercepts 2/3 and 4), then bridges court involvement into a recovery continuum with continuing care.

## Core Workflow: 90-Day Stabilization + 12-Month Continuing Care

### Days 1-7: Intake & Assessment
- SUD screening, OUD risk assessment, education status review, family engagement assessment
- Ages 16+ with suspected OUD referred for MOUD clinical assessment linkage

### Days 8-30: Stabilization
- Weekly contact cadence; individualized recovery plan; school reentry/GED coordination
- Family education on fentanyl/naloxone/MOUD; SCBF begins; naloxone distributed

### Days 31-60: Active Recovery Navigation
- Vocational exploration, prosocial activities, mutual support; home visits
- 30-day milestone assessment; MOUD adherence monitoring for clinical-track youth

### Days 61-90: Transition Planning
- Long-term education placement; employment; community recovery supports
- 90-day completion assessment; warm handoffs; outcome documentation

### Months 4-15: Youth Recovery Community (YRC)
- APG-informed continuing care for up to 12 months post-stabilization
- Decreasing intensity; crisis re-engagement capacity
