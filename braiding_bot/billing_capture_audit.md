# Billing Capture Audit

> **Frequency**: Monthly
> **Completed by**: Program Director + fiscal staff
> **Purpose**: Track total CM interactions documented vs. total billed. The gap between documented and billed represents invisible labor — work being done that no funder is paying for. This audit makes that labor visible and quantifiable.
